Meatless Mondays

Do well for the environment and yourself by going meatless on Mondays!

  • How does going meatless help the planet?
    • Reduces human-made greenhouse gas emmissions
    • Saves water
    • Decrease fossil fuel usage

  • How does going meatless help YOU?
    • Reduce risks of chronic, preventable diseases
    • Fight weight gain
    • Increase chances of living longer
    • Better nutrition
