Roasted Corn and Black Bean Salad

Yum! This dish has a southwestern flair, and tastes amazing on a bed of lettuce, with chips, or all on its own.

What you'll need:

  • Salad:
    • 2 cobs corn, cut kernels from cob, or use 1 cup frozen/thawed sweet corn
    • 1 1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
    • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
    • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded, finely chopped
    • 1 small red bell pepper, chopped
    • 1-2 cloves of minced garlic
    • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 large or two small avocados, peeled and cut into small dice
    • 1 cup quartered cherry tomatoes
  • Dressing:
    • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
    • 2 1/2 tablespoons light olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
    • 1/2-1 teaspoon sugar

What you'll do:

  1. In medium bowl, combine corn and oil, toss to coat and lightly salt.
  2. Spread corn in ungreased baking pan.
  3. Bake in a preheated 425 degree oven for about 15 minutes or until corn begins to turn light golden brown, stirring every 5 minutes or so.
  4. Cool 10 minutes. (Or you could do this stove-top method in large 12 inch skillet on medium high heat).
  5. Meanwhile prepare dressing. Combine all ingredients in a small jar with tight fitting lid and shake until well blended.
  6. In a large bowl combine the cooled corn, cilantro, red onion, jalapeno pepper, red bell pepper, garlic, black beans, avocados and fresh tomatoes.
  7. Mix well and add dressing, and then toss again.
  8. Serve immediately or refrigerate up to 24 hours (The ingredients marinate one another overnight - it tastes even better that way).
