Huevos Rancheros

Breaking the nighttime fast with a protein-rich, tasty dish. Plates well with salsa, avocado, and lettuce :)

What you'll need:

  • 1/4 cup neutral oil, like grapeseed or corn, plus a little more
  • Four 5-inch corn tortillas
  • 1/4 cup refried beans, or any soft well-seasoned beans
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup queso fresco or grated Monterey Jack or cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

What you'll do:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Heat oil in a small skillet over medium heat. When hot, fry tortillas one at a time until softened and heated through, about 3 seconds per side. Make sure they do not crisp. Drain on paper towels.
  3. Spread 1 tablespoon of beans in center of each tortilla and set aside. (You can prepare this dish to this point up to one hour in advance).
  4. Use a little more oil to fry eggs sunny side up, sprinkling salt and pepper while they cook to your liking.
  5. Put an egg in the center of each tortilla, then top with 2 tablespoons salsa and 1 tablespoon cheese.
  6. Carefully transfer tortillas to baking dish that holds them snugly.
  7. Bake until the cheese is melted, about 5 minutes. Serve immediately!
